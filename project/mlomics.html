MLOmics: Cancer Multi-Omics Benchmark

Zheng Chen1, Ziwei Yang2
1Osaka University, 2Kyoto University
arXiv Code Data

Abstract

We introduce MLOmics, a comprehensive benchmarking framework for cancer multi-omics analysis. Our dataset spans multiple omics types (mRNA, miRNA, methylation, CNV) and supports a variety of tasks, including classification, clustering, and imputation.

We curate over 30 real-world tasks across different cancer types to encourage robust evaluation of machine learning methods in precision oncology.
